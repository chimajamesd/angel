About Us

Holy Ghost Revival Royal Assembly is a vibrant, spirit-filled church dedicated to spreading the love, power, and message of Jesus Christ. Our mission is to ignite revival in hearts and communities through passionate worship, sound teaching, and compassionate outreach.

Our Vision

To raise a generation empowered by the Holy Spirit, living in holiness, and manifesting the glory of God in every sphere of life.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the Trinity, the authority of the Bible, the power of prayer, and the necessity of a personal relationship with Jesus Christ. We are committed to fostering spiritual growth, unity, and service.

Join Us

Whether you are seeking a church home or exploring faith, you are welcome at Holy Ghost Revival Royal Assembly. Experience dynamic worship, inspiring messages, and a loving community.
